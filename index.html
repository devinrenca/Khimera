Building Tools That Put Humans First

We are dedicated to building digital tools dedicated to reshaping how people interact with technology. Our mission is focused on creating tools that empower individuals to take control of their digital lives while fostering healthier, more intentional experiences, across all devices.

By combining innovation with thoughtful, human-centered design, we build solutions that prioritize balance, focus, and meaningful engagement. We firmly believe that the future of technology will be built around enabling everyday people to build healthier, happier lives.

The Future Of Tech Is Accountable

Technology can be a tool used to improve our human experience, help to solve problems and boost human creativity. But modern day tech solutions are creating more questions than answers. Our tools help to declutter the digital landscape by providing solutions that can identify synthentic AI generated content, block online ads & tracking cookies, encouraging healthier digital habits and help to secure personal data.

Through continuous research and development, we strive to redefine the relationship between humans and the digital world, allowing for tech that is more mindful, productive, and stable.

Data Transparency At Every Step

In an age of data misuse, transparency is at the heart of Khimera's mission. Whether on mobile or desktop, our applications never archive or sell user data. Instead, the upcoming Digital Wellbeing toolkit allows users to identify how devices, sites and applications in their tech ecosystem are using personal data.

Our platforms leverage localized user analytics, that, when combined with a suite of interactive widgets, can work to demystify the challenge of managing data in our digital lives. This process allows us to uphold user privacy, while providing our users the capacity to transform how they think about, and manage personal data.
